TITLE
Diu Diu Bear's Modular AWM

SHORT DESCRIPTION
Adds a powerful modular AWM, eight attachments, .338 Red Magnum ammunition, scope support, and Barman shop availability.

FULL WORKSHOP DESCRIPTION
Build a heavy bolt-action rifle around a modular AWM receiver and tune it with dedicated back, front, and magazine assemblies.

FEATURES
- AWM receiver with 200 base damage.
- Basic, Short, and Devil back assemblies.
- Basic, Compressor, and Compressor Long front assemblies.
- Basic 5-round and Long 10-round magazines.
- Compatible with standard scopes and optics.
- Vanilla R700 firing and reload sounds with custom suppressed audio.
- .338 Red Magnum ammunition with maximum armour penetration.
